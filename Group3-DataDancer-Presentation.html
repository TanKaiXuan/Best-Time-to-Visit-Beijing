Introduction

Background

Here we present an intuitive Shiny App to visualize the weather data of Beijing City. There are several features used to describe the weather of Beijing. We will find the optimal time to visit Beijing based on personal choices.

  • Shiny App: https://stephen-cheng.shinyapps.io/Best-Time-to-Visit-Beijing/
  • Github Code: https://github.com/jetrobert/Best-Time-to-Visit-Beijing
  • Data source: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Beijing+PM2.5+Data#

Problem Statement

My friends and I are planning to visit Beijing. The weather in Beijing, however, is different compared to Malaysia. Therefore, we want to identify the best time to visit Beijing.

Objective

  • To gain insight into Beijings historical meteorological data.

  • To identify the best time to visit Beijing.

Data Collection

Our dataset is obtained from UCI https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Beijing+PM2.5+Data

  • The features of our dataset are as follows:

  1. PM2.5: Particulate Matter 2.5,

  2. DEWP: Dew Point,

  3. TEMP: Temperature,

  4. PRES: Pressure,

  5. cbwd: Combined wind direction,

  6. Iws: Cumulated wind speed (m/s),

  7. Is: Cumulated hours of snow,

  8. Ir: Cumulated hours of rain.
